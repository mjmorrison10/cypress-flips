Quick answers before you drive

Common questions from buyers and vendors

Used goods should not require blind trust. Here are the answers people usually want before meeting near Cypress, CA.

Are website prices lower than Facebook, OfferUp, eBay, or Mercari?

Yes. Website prices are intended to be my best direct local pickup prices because there are no marketplace fees, shipping costs, or platform markups.

Can I inspect the item before buying?

Yes. For local pickup, I expect buyers to inspect the item before finalizing the deal. I would rather you feel confident than rushed.

Are items tested?

If a listing does not say untested, assume it has been tested to the best of my ability. If something is untested, I put that in the title/description instead of hiding it.

Where do meetups happen?

Cypress, CA is home base. Public meetups around Cypress, Orange County, and the LA area are preferred. Delivery may be possible when the deal supports it.

Do you buy inventory from vendors or local sellers?

Yes. Send photos, quantity, rough condition, and the price you need. If the items can realistically sell online and the numbers work for both sides, I can make an offer.
