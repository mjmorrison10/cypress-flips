# Cypress Flips Negotiation & Messaging Scripts

Use these as copy/paste replies across Facebook Marketplace, OfferUp, eBay, Mercari, Nextdoor, and text messages.

Tone: honest, friendly, firm, no-pressure.

---

## Universal replies

### “Is this still available?”
Yes, it is currently available. I’m based in Cypress, CA. Feel free to ask anything about condition or pickup before meeting.

### “What’s your lowest?”
I’m open to fair offers. My website direct local price is already lower than my marketplace ask because there are no platform fees or shipping. What price were you thinking?

### “Can you do [lowball]?”
I appreciate the offer, but I’m not there right now. I price based on condition, included items, and current resale value. If you can meet near Cypress and make pickup easy, I may have some flexibility.

### “Can you hold it?”
I can place it on hold for a confirmed meetup window. If plans change, please let me know so I can keep the listing accurate.

### “Where are you located?”
I’m based in Cypress, CA. I usually prefer public meetups around Cypress/Orange County for everyone’s safety.

### “Can you deliver?”
Possibly, depending on distance and item price. Delivery fees would need to be agreed on before I drive out.

### “Why is it cheaper on your website?”
Website direct local prices are lower because there are no marketplace fees, shipping costs, or platform markups. Marketplaces are priced higher to allow for fees and negotiation.

## Condition / trust replies

### “Does it work?”
If the listing does not say untested, it has been tested to the best of my ability. I’m happy to clarify exactly what was tested for this specific item.

### “Any damage?”
Known flaws are listed in the description and shown in photos when possible. If you want a close-up of a specific area, I can send extra photos before you drive out.

### “Is it complete?”
Only the items shown/described are included. If I’m not confident something is complete, I do not call it complete.

### “Can I inspect it before buying?”
Yes. For local pickup, I expect buyers to inspect the item before finalizing the deal. I would rather you feel confident before buying.

### “Can I return it?”
Refunds are handled case by case. If I missed a material issue that should have been disclosed, I will work with you to make it right.

## Parent buyer replies

### Parent buying a Switch/console
If this is for your kid, I’m happy to walk through exactly what’s included and any condition issues before you drive out. I try to disclose flaws up front so there are no surprises.

### Parent concerned about condition
Totally understandable. Used electronics can be a gamble if they’re not inspected. I list known flaws honestly and can send extra photos if you want to check anything specific.

### Parent asking for discount
I can be flexible if the situation makes sense and pickup is easy. Make me a fair offer and I’ll let you know what I can do.

## Collector replies

### “Does it have the box?”
If a box is not shown in the photos or mentioned in the listing, assume it does not include the box.

### “Is it mint?”
I avoid calling used/displayed items mint unless they truly are. Please review photos and condition notes. I can send close-ups if you need them.

### “Is this rare?”
I try not to overuse the word rare. Some items are harder to find locally or retired/vintage, but I price based on actual condition and current comps.

## Video game replies

### Switch digital games question
No physical games are included unless shown separately. Any digital games/apps shown on the screen are not guaranteed because digital purchases are usually tied to a Nintendo account.

### Loose cartridge question
This is cartridge only. No case, manual, or inserts unless shown/described.

### Game Boy button issue
The buttons work, but because it’s vintage hardware they may require a firmer press at times. That is disclosed in the listing so buyers know before meeting.

## Plush replies

### “Does it have the tag?”
Yes, if the tag is shown in the photos/listing. Please note any tag bending or wear described.

### “Can you bundle plush?”
Yes, I can usually work a better price if you bundle multiple plush/items and meet near Cypress.

## Vendor / liquidation replies

### Vendor asks if you buy inventory
Yes, I may be interested. Send photos, quantity, rough condition, and what price you need. If it can realistically sell online and the numbers work for both of us, I can make an offer.

### Vendor wants quick cash
That’s exactly the kind of deal I’m open to. I’m comfortable waiting for items to sell if the buy-in price makes sense. Send photos/list and I’ll review.

### Vendor has random inventory
I’m open to almost anything if it can sell online and there’s enough margin. Video games, collectibles, TCG, electronics, toys, plush, motorcycle gear, and accessories are all worth sending over.

### Vendor asks if you’ll buy everything
Possibly. I can buy individual items, small lots, or larger batches if the numbers make sense. Send photos/list and the price you’re looking for.
