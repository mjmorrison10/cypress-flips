---

## Strategy for Plush / Disney / Soft Goods

### Primary buyers

1. **Disney collectors**
   - Care about character, tag, Disney Store connection, vintage/retired status, and condition.
   - Best copy angle: “tag attached,” “Disney Store,” “vintage,” “collector/display piece,” “condition shown clearly.”

2. **Parents / gift buyers**
   - Care about cuteness, affordability, cleanliness, and whether it is a good small gift.
   - Best copy angle: “cute gift,” “affordable pickup,” “Disney/Pokémon fan,” “local Cypress pickup.”

3. **Nostalgia buyers**
   - Care about character memories: Bambi, Pinocchio, Lion King II, Lady and the Tramp II, Mandalorian/Grogu.
   - Best copy angle: “late-90s/early-2000s nostalgia,” “classic Disney character,” “harder-to-find character.”

4. **Bundle buyers**
   - Plush buyers often respond well to “bundle discounts.”
   - Best copy angle: “I can bundle with other Disney plush if available.”

---

## Platform Pricing Philosophy

Website prices are your direct local pickup prices. Marketplace listings should generally be higher because plush buyers often negotiate and because shipped plush requires packaging/time.

| Platform | Pricing Strategy |
|---|---|
| Website | Lowest direct local price |
| Facebook Marketplace | +15–35% ask, local pickup, bundle-friendly |
| OfferUp | +15–35% ask, short mobile copy |
| Mercari | +20–50% depending character, because plush performs well there |
| eBay | +20–50% depending rarity/tag, buyer pays shipping |
| Nextdoor | Softer family/gift positioning |

---

## Plush Listing Rules

### Always include

- Character name
- Franchise / movie
- Disney Store / official tag if visible
- Tag attached or not attached
- Known lint, marks, staining, tag wear, or missing info
- Cypress, CA pickup for local platforms
- “See photos”

### Recommended prep before photos/shipping

- Lint roll gently.
- Photograph front, side, back, tag, and any flaws.
- Do not claim smoke-free/pet-free unless true.
- If selling a vintage plush, mention that normal age/display wear should be expected.

## Photo Notes for Plush Listings

For future plush listings, try to photograph:

1. Front full body
2. Side angle
3. Back angle
4. Tag closeup
5. Any flaw closeup
6. Size reference if possible

For black plush like Figaro, use bright light and lint roll first so the photos look cleaner.
