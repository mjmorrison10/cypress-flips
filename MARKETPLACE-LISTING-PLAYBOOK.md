> Purpose: This document turns the website inventory into marketplace-ready listings for Facebook Marketplace, OfferUp, eBay, Mercari, and other Southern California channels. It is written for fast upload, honest condition disclosure, and higher conversion.

---

## 1. Platform Strategy: What Works Where

### Facebook Marketplace
**Best for:** local pickups, parents, bundles, consoles, plush, collectibles, motorcycle gear.  
**Sales style:** trust + local convenience + clear flaws + quick pickup.  
**Tactics:**
- Put the main item and strongest buying reason in the title.
- Mention **Cypress, CA** early.
- Keep descriptions human and local.
- Use “public meetup” and “condition disclosed” to build trust.
- Price slightly negotiable if you are willing.
- Use 8–10 photos when possible.

### OfferUp
**Best for:** quick local sale, price-conscious buyers, parents, video games, electronics.  
**Sales style:** shorter, direct, mobile-first.  
**Tactics:**
- Short title with keywords.
- Description should be compact and scannable.
- Mention Cypress pickup.
- Lead with “works,” “tested,” “OEM,” “cartridge only,” or “tag attached.”

### eBay
**Best for:** collectors, niche plush, anime figures, video games, harder-to-find items.  
**Sales style:** SEO title + exact condition + shipping-friendly language.  
**Tactics:**
- Title should be keyword-dense, usually 70–80 characters.
- Condition field matters. Use “Used,” “New,” “New other,” or “For parts/untested” honestly.
- Use item specifics whenever possible.
- Include “See photos” and detailed condition notes.
- Price higher than local platforms to account for shipping, fees, and wider buyer pool.

### Mercari
**Best for:** plush, anime figures, Disney collectibles, smaller video games.  
**Sales style:** friendly, collectible-focused, gift-friendly.  
**Tactics:**
- Short title, clean description.
- “Smoke-free home” only if true; otherwise do not claim it.
- Mention tag attached, cartridge only, packaging wear, etc.
- Mercari buyers like bundles; offer bundle discounts if you want.

### Craigslist
**Best for:** consoles, electronics, bulk lots, local buyers who do not use FB/OfferUp.  
**Sales style:** functional and direct.  
**Tactics:**
- Use practical titles.
- Include location and pickup terms.
- Less emotional copy; more facts.

### Nextdoor
**Best for:** parents, neighbors, local trust, family-friendly items, consoles, plush.  
**Sales style:** community trust.  
**Tactics:**
- Mention Cypress / local pickup.
- Lead with safety and honesty.
- Best for parent-friendly bundles and kids’ gifts.

### Other Southern California Channels Worth Testing
- **Mercari Local**: good for smaller collectibles/plush and customers who prefer app-based purchase flow.
- **Whatnot**: useful later for TCG cards, collectibles, anime figures, Disney lots, video game lots, live selling.
- **Craigslist Orange County / LA**: older platform, still useful for consoles, electronics, and bulk liquidation.
- **Nextdoor**: local parent/family audience; good for Switch bundles, plush, Disney, kids’ items.
- **Reddit local groups**: r/OrangeCounty, r/GameSale, r/GameSwap. Use carefully and follow rules.
- **TCGplayer / eBay**: when Pokémon/TCG inventory becomes a major category.
- **Reverb / CycleTrader / Facebook motorcycle groups**: for future motorcycle accessories.

## 2. Global Rules for All Listings

### Always Include
- Cypress, CA pickup / meetup area.
- Condition summary.
- What is included.
- What is not included.
- Whether tested or untested.
- Known flaws.
- “See photos.”

### Do Not Claim Unless True
- Smoke-free home.
- Pet-free home.
- Fully tested.
- Complete.
- Authentic / official unless you are confident.
- Rare unless market supports it.

### Local CTA Templates
Use one of these endings:

**Buyer-focused:**  
“Available for local pickup near Cypress, CA. Feel free to ask questions before meeting.”

**Parent-friendly:**  
“Great option for a kid/family setup. I’m happy to answer questions before you drive out.”

**Collector-focused:**  
“Collector/display condition noted honestly. Please review photos and ask any questions.”

**Vendor sourcing:**  
“Follow Cypress Flips for more local finds, collectibles, games, and electronics.”

# 5. Marketplace Upload Checklist

For every listing:

- Upload best front/main image first.
- Use all available angles.
- Put defects/condition issues in the description, not only photos.
- Add Cypress, CA pickup language.
- Mention “cash/Zelle/marketplace checkout” only if you accept them.
- Do not claim tested unless tested.
- Use “READ” in eBay titles for major flaws like cracked Joy-Con, untested electronics, or packaging damage.
- For eBay, measure/weight before choosing shipping.
- For plush, lint roll before photographing/shipping.
- For electronics/consoles, photograph powered-on proof when possible.
- For cartridge-only games, photograph front label and back contacts if possible.

# 8. Quick Local Message Templates

### Buyer asks: “Is this available?”
“Yes, it is currently available. I’m based in Cypress, CA. Let me know if you want to ask anything about condition or set up a public meetup.”

### Buyer asks for lower price
“I’m open to fair offers. I inspect and disclose condition carefully, so the price reflects what’s included and any flaws. What price were you thinking?”

### Buyer asks if item works
“If the listing does not say untested, assume it was tested to the best of my ability. I’m happy to clarify exactly what was tested for this item.”

### Vendor asks if you buy inventory
“Yes, I may be interested. Send photos, quantity, rough condition, and what price you need. If it can sell online and the numbers make sense for both of us, I can make an offer.”
