Prepared for Cypress Flips / Michael Morrison  
Local anchor: Cypress, CA • Orange County • Los Angeles area

This is Batch 1 of the category-by-category marketplace system. It focuses on video games and consoles because this is currently the strongest revenue category and likely the fastest-moving inventory locally.

---

## Strategy for Video Games & Consoles

### Primary buyers

1. **Parents buying for kids**
   - They care about working condition, included accessories, safety, and value.
   - They do not want to accidentally buy a broken or incomplete console.
   - Best copy angle: “tested / powered on / complete starter setup / OEM accessories / honest cosmetic flaws.”

2. **Retro gamers and collectors**
   - They care about authenticity, condition, completeness, nostalgia, game library, and whether the console/game is tested.
   - Best copy angle: “original hardware / cartridge only / label wear disclosed / nostalgic bundle / real photos.”

3. **Local deal hunters**
   - They compare Facebook, OfferUp, and eBay.
   - They want to feel like direct local pickup is cheaper than marketplace checkout.
   - Best copy angle: “website direct price is lower, local Cypress pickup, ask questions before driving.”

4. **Gift buyers**
   - They need confidence quickly.
   - Best copy angle: “good starter bundle,” “ready to play,” “great birthday/holiday gift,” while still being honest about flaws.

---

## Platform Pricing Philosophy

Website prices are intentionally the best direct local pickup prices. Marketplace prices should be higher because they include:

- platform fees
- negotiation room
- more buyer exposure
- shipping risk/time if applicable
- marketplace buyers expecting offers/counteroffers

Recommended general markup:

| Platform | Pricing Strategy |
|---|---|
| Website | Lowest direct local price |
| Facebook Marketplace | +10–20% ask, accept website price for easy buyer |
| OfferUp | +10–20% ask, expect negotiation |
| eBay | +15–35% depending item + buyer pays shipping |
| Mercari | +15–30%, buyer-friendly wording |
| Nextdoor | Similar to Facebook but softer, family/community wording |
| Craigslist | Similar to Facebook but more direct/factual |

---

## General Video Game Listing Rules

### Always include

- “Cypress, CA pickup”
- whether it is tested/powered on
- exactly what is included
- exactly what is not included
- cosmetic flaws
- digital games/apps not guaranteed if on Switch home screen
- cartridge only / no case for loose games
- local pickup/public meetup language

### Avoid

- “Mint” unless truly mint
- “Complete” unless every expected accessory/manual/insert is verified
- “Rare” unless market supports it
- claiming digital games are included
- hiding cosmetic defects

## Message Scripts for Video Games

### “Is this still available?”
“Yes, it is currently available. I’m based in Cypress, CA. I inspect and disclose condition before listing, so feel free to ask anything before meeting.”

### “Does it work?”
“Yes, if the listing does not say untested, it has been tested to the best of my ability. For this item specifically, I can clarify exactly what was tested.”

### “Can you do less?”
“I’m open to fair offers. My website direct local price is already lower than my marketplace ask because there are no platform fees/shipping. What price were you thinking?”

### “Can you hold it?”
“I can hold it for a confirmed meetup window. If plans change, just let me know so I can keep the listing accurate.”

### Parent buyer message
“If this is for your kid, I’m happy to walk through what’s included, what condition issues exist, and whether it makes sense before you drive out.”
