---

## Strategy for Action Figures & Collectible Statues

### Primary buyers

1. **Anime collectors**
   - Care about franchise, character, figure line, display pose, box/no-box status, and condition.
   - Best copy angle: “display-ready,” “great shelf presence,” “condition shown clearly,” “no box unless pictured.”

2. **One Piece collectors**
   - Character names matter more than generic “anime figure.”
   - Use exact names: Roronoa Zoro, Trafalgar Law, Charlotte Katakuri.
   - Include line names: King of Artist, Battle Record Collection, Ichibansho.

3. **Jujutsu Kaisen collectors**
   - Character + Banpresto line is important.
   - Yuji and Megumi buyers are usually looking for affordable display pieces.

4. **WWE / wrestling collectors**
   - Care about wrestler name, series, packaging condition, and accessories.
   - Cody Rhodes has strong name demand, but this specific Knuckle Crunchers item is lower-ticket and packaging condition matters heavily.

5. **Gift buyers**
   - Parents/friends may search simply for “anime figure,” “One Piece figure,” “Jujutsu Kaisen figure,” or “WWE Cody Rhodes toy.”
   - Keep local platform copy simple enough for non-collectors.

---

## General Rules for Figure Listings

### Always include

- Character name
- Franchise
- Figure line / brand if known
- Box included or not included
- Condition notes
- Display wear / scuffs / missing pieces
- Whether accessories/base/stand are included
- Cypress, CA pickup for local listings

### Avoid

- “Mint” unless truly mint and boxed
- “Complete” unless every accessory/stand/part is verified
- “Rare” unless the market supports it
- Saying “new” when item is displayed/opened
- Hiding packaging damage or clearance stickers

### Strong figure keywords

```text
Banpresto
Bandai Spirits
Ichibansho
King of Artist
Battle Record Collection
One Piece
Jujutsu Kaisen

display figure
anime statue
collector figure
```

## Pricing Philosophy

Website prices are your lowest direct local pickup prices. Marketplace listings should leave room for negotiation and platform fees.

| Platform | Pricing Strategy |
|---|---|
| Website | Lowest direct local pickup price |
| Facebook Marketplace | +15–35% ask, accept website price for easy local pickup |
| OfferUp | +15–35% ask, expect negotiation |
| eBay | +25–50% for niche collectors, buyer pays shipping |
| Mercari | +20–45%, friendly collectible/gift copy |

“If a box is not shown in the photos or mentioned in the listing, assume it does not include the box. I try to make included items clear before meetup.”

### “Any damage?”
“I list known flaws in the description and show the item from multiple angles. I can send more photos before you drive out if you want to confirm anything specific.”

### “Can you hold it?”
“I can place it on hold for a confirmed meetup window. If anything changes, just let me know so I can update the listing.”

“Yes, I can usually work a better direct local price if you bundle multiple figures and meet near Cypress.”
